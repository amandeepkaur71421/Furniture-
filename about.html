Our Story

Its all started in 1990

Welcome to Indian Brasserie. Our journey began in 1990 with a simple vision: to spark romance and bring families closer together over the vibrant, aromatic wonders of authentic Indian cuisine.

For over three decades, our candlelit tables have set the stage for unforgettable date nights, while our warm atmosphere welcomes generations of families.

We handcraft every mouth-watering dish—from rich, slow-simmered curries to smoky tandoori delights—using only the freshest locally sourced ingredients and time-honored spice blends. Whether you are sharing a quiet, intimate toast for two or celebrating a lively milestone with the whole family, our 65-seat dining room is your home away from home.

Our Goals

Welcome to Indian Brasserie. We are dedicated to serving exceptional food and beverages that honor the highest standards of quality, freshness, and seasonality. By seamlessly blending modern-creative innovation with classic Indian cooking traditions, we create a menu that honors our past while embracing the future.

Our team approaches every service with warmth, graciousness, efficiency, and absolute integrity. We believe that true hospitality is a skill, and we practice it with professional pride every single day. Our ultimate goal is simple: to ensure every guest who enters our 65-seat dining room leaves deeply impressed by their experience and genuinely excited to return.

Fresh and delicious traditional Bangladeshi food to delight the whole family.
