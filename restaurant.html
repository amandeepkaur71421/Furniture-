Spiced Cottage
Cheese Skewers

Savor the authentic taste of the clay oven with our signature Paneer Tikka. Pillowy cubes of fresh cottage cheese are infused with aromatic tandoori spices and layered on skewers with charred onions and crisp green peppers. This smoky starter comes beautifully paired with vibrant green chutney and a sweet, tangy dipping sauce to elevate every bite.
